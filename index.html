Equator: Expense Tracker

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Welcome to Equator: Expense Tracker. This document governs your use of the App and outlines our privacy practices. By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to be bound by these terms. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Your Data is Yours

We built Equator with strict privacy principles. We do not want your personal or financial data, and we do not have access to it.

  • Local Storage: All financial data is stored entirely locally on your device using Apple's native database frameworks. We do not host your data on our servers.
  • iCloud Backup & Sync: The App provides an optional backup feature that saves JSON snapshot files to your personal Apple iCloud Drive. These backups are initiated by you and stored in your private iCloud container. We do not have access to your iCloud account.
  • Third-Party APIs: The App connects to the internet to fetch public, live currency exchange rates. Your device's IP address may be temporarily exposed to third-party service providers. This data is never linked to your personal identity or local financial data.
  • On-Device Intelligence: The App uses Apple's on-device machine learning frameworks for convenience features. All processing occurs entirely on your device. No data is sent to any external server.
  • Analytics and Tracking: We do not track your usage behaviour and we do not sell, rent, or trade any data to third parties.
  • Children's Privacy: The App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

2. Terms of Service

The App is designed to be a personal utility for tracking your day-to-day finances and expenses. It is not a substitute for professional financial, accounting, or tax advice. The information, calculations, and summaries provided are for personal reference and convenience only. You are solely responsible for your financial decisions.

3. Data Backup

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you are entirely responsible for backing it up. We cannot recover lost, deleted, or corrupted data.
