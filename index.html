Yes. Your transactions, accounts, budgets, and portfolio are stored entirely on your device. Your data is never transmitted to our servers except when you actively use the AI Chat feature, which sends your recent financial data to our server and Google’s Gemini API to generate a response. Every other feature of the app works entirely offline. Full details are in the Privacy Policy below.
When you send a message in AI Chat, your transactions, accounts, budgets, and portfolio data from the last 6 months are transmitted to our server and forwarded to Google’s Gemini API to generate a response. We do not send your name, email, Apple ID, IP address, or device identifier to Google. Our server does not store your data. You can disable AI Chat anytime in Settings → Privacy. For full details, see Section 1.7 of the Privacy Policy.
Backup is available with Equator Pro or Equator Premium. Go to Settings → Data & Backup to export a backup file to your iCloud Drive. To restore, import the same file from the same screen. Free users can still export transactions as CSV once they upgrade to Pro.
Free: up to 3 accounts, 3 budgets, 3 recurring transactions, 1 portfolio ticker (24h refresh), and 5 AI Chat prompts per day.

Pro (one-time SGD 3.98): unlimited accounts, budgets, and recurring transactions. iCloud backup and CSV export unlocked. Same AI Chat and portfolio limits as Free.

Premium (SGD 3.98/month): everything in Pro, plus 200 AI Chat prompts per day, unlimited portfolio tickers, and faster price refreshes (every 10 minutes).
Premium is managed through Apple. Open your iPhone’s Settings, tap your name at the top, tap Subscriptions, and select Equator Premium to cancel. You will continue to have access until the end of the current billing period. We cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf.
Go to Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase Pro originally. The same applies to active Premium subscriptions.
All refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the purchase, and tap Request a Refund. We do not have the ability to issue refunds directly.
Equator supports a wide range of global currencies for your accounts and transactions. Live exchange rates are provided for your home currency plus 17 major world currencies. You can set your home currency in Settings → General.
All your data is stored locally on your device, so deleting the app permanently removes it. If you saved a backup to iCloud Drive before deleting, you can reinstall and restore from that file. We strongly recommend exporting a backup regularly via Settings → Data & Backup (requires Pro or Premium).
Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all local data. To also remove backups, delete them from within the app’s Data & Backup settings or directly from your iCloud Drive. Because we do not have servers that store your personal financial data, there is nothing for us to delete on our end.
Subscription terms Equator Premium is a SGD 3.98/month auto-renewing subscription (or equivalent in your local currency). It renews automatically at the end of each monthly period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the current period ends. Manage or cancel in your iPhone’s Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.
